About Our Charity

Our mission is to provide support to those who are struggling with poverty, hunger, and lack of education, and we do this by working closely with local communities and organizations. We also provide shelter to those who are in need, whether they are homeless or have been displaced by natural disasters. We are committed to transparency and accountability, and we take great care to ensure that every dollar we raise goes directly to those in need. We are constantly looking for new ways to expand our reach and impact, and we welcome any and all support from individuals and organizations who share our goal of improving the lives of those in Pakistan.

Our Mission

Our mission as a charity is to empower communities and improve lives by providing access to essential resources and opportunities. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to reach their full potential and lead a successful life, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our focus is on addressing the most pressing needs of those in need, including access to education, healthcare, and basic needs like food and shelter. We work closely with local communities to understand their unique challenges and to develop solutions that are tailored to meet their needs. Through our efforts, we aim to create a world where everyone has access to the resources they need to thrive and reach their full potential. We believe that by working together, we can make a positive and lasting impact, and build a better future for all.

Our Vision

Our vision as a charity is to improve the lives of the people of Pakistan, with a special focus on the poor communities of Balochistan. We believe that everyone deserves access to the resources and opportunities they need to lead successful and fulfilling lives. We are committed to working with local communities to address their unique needs and challenges, and to provide them with the support they need to thrive. We see a future where the people of Balochistan have access to quality education, healthcare, and basic needs, and where they are empowered to make their own decisions and reach their full potential. We believe that by working together, we can create a brighter future for the people of Balochistan, and for Pakistan as a whole.

No Proper Education

Education is a major cause of concern in Pakistan, with many children not having access to quality education. This is due to a lack of schools, trained teachers, and resources in many areas of the country. According to a report by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), the literacy rate in Pakistan is only 58%, with a significant gap between the literacy rates of men and women.

FLOODING IN PAK

Flooding is a major problem in Pakistan, and it causes significant damage to the country's infrastructure and economy. The monsoon season in Pakistan typically runs from July to September, and heavy rainfall can cause rivers and streams to overflow, leading to widespread flooding.The areas most affected by flooding in Pakistan are typically the low-lying areas along the Indus River and its tributaries.

Basic Needs

Access to food is a major issue in Pakistan, where poverty and food insecurity are widespread. Despite being an agricultural country, many people are unable to access enough nutritious food to meet their basic needs. This can lead to malnutrition and other health problems.
